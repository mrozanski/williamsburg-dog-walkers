About Monica

From the creators of The Williamsburg Cat Sitter, and by popular demand, we proudly present: The Williamsburg Dog Walkers!

Monica has always been an animal lover for as long as she can remember, and while she may have earned the tite of “crazy cat lady,” her love for dogs runs just as deep. After years of caring for her Persian Doll Face kitty Uma and building a trusted name in the cat-sitting world, Monica began receiving frequent requests from dog parents who were looking for the same level of care, professionalism, and heart for their pups.

Inspired by her deep bond with animals and the incredible community she’s connected with through cat sitting, Monica knew it was time to expand her mission. And so, The Williamsburg Dog Walkers was born — an extension of the same values, attention to detail, and genuine love that made her a favorite among cat lovers.

To meet the growing needs of local dog families, Monica has built a small, trusted team of fellow dog lovers who share her dedication and passion. Each team member is carefully chosen and trained to ensure your pup receives the same thoughtful, loving care that Monica has become known for.

Whether it’s a neighborhood stroll, a park adventure, or just some extra cuddles while you’re away, The Williamsburg Dog Walkers is here to treat your pup like family.

Why Us?

I have a deep love for dogs and know the trust it takes to leave your pup in someone else’s care. As a Williamsburg local, I’m nearby and available, even on short notice. With the help of my amazing team of dog walkers, we treat every dog like family, ensuring they get the exercise, love, and attention they need while you’re away.
